zKillboard is a collaborative effort by Karbowiak and Squizz Caphinator. At present it is completely populated by API verified kills obtained from EVE-KILL. If you'd like to see your kills here please provide your API key at this location: http://eve-kill.net/?a=apikey You can find the source for zKillboard at https://github.com/EVE-KILL/zKillboard
